KitchenForge Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

KitchenForge ("the App") is a food pantry management and recipe discovery application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is used.

Data Collection

KitchenForge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All pantry items, recipes, preferences, and spending data are stored locally on your device only.

Permissions Used

  • Camera: Used only to scan food items when you choose to use the camera feature. Camera images are processed on-device and are never uploaded, stored, or shared.
  • Microphone: Used only for voice-to-text input when you choose to add items by speaking. Audio is processed by your device's built-in speech recognition and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted by the App.
  • Internet: Used to search for online recipes from TheMealDB, a free recipe database. No personal information is sent in these requests.

Third-Party Services

The App uses TheMealDB API (themealdb.com) to provide online recipe search and discovery. When you search for recipes, only your search query is sent to TheMealDB. No personal information, device identifiers, or usage data is transmitted.

Data Storage

All app data (pantry items, spending history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using browser local storage. This data is not backed up to any cloud service and is only accessible by the App on your device.

Children's Privacy

KitchenForge does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not require account creation or personal information of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing.
