APP NAME: KitchenForge - Smart Pantry & Recipes

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Track food, get recipes from what you have, save money, waste nothing.

FULL DESCRIPTION:
KitchenForge is your personal kitchen assistant that tracks everything in your pantry, suggests recipes based on what you already have, and makes sure nothing goes to waste.

ADD FOOD 4 EASY WAYS
- Camera Scan: Point at your fridge and AI detects every item
- Receipt Photo: Snap your grocery receipt to import items instantly
- Voice: Say "Add milk that expires in 7 days" and it's done
- Manual: Quick-add with name, quantity, cost, and expiry

SMART PANTRY TRACKING
- See all 47+ items at a glance with beautiful cards
- Color-coded expiry indicators (red/yellow/green)
- Search and filter by category (Produce, Dairy, Meat, Frozen)
- Edit quantities, costs, and expiry dates with one tap
- Total pantry value always visible

RECIPE MATCHING
- 10+ recipes sorted by how many ingredients you already own
- Filter by cuisine: Italian, Asian, Indian, Mexican, Thai
- Sort by best match, fastest cook time, or fewest missing items
- Full step-by-step instructions with ingredient checklists
- Start Cooking mode with built-in timer and checkable steps

EXPIRATION RADAR
- Push notifications before food expires
- Customizable reminder window (1-7 days before)
- "Expiring This Week" carousel on your dashboard
- Recipe suggestions that use expiring items first

COST TRACKING
- Track the cost of every item you add
- Daily, weekly, and monthly spending dashboard
- See your total pantry value at a glance
- Visual spending breakdown with progress bar

ZERO WASTE
- Never forget what's in your fridge again
- Get recipe ideas that use what you have
- One-tap "Add missing items to shopping list"
- Mark ingredients as used after cooking

Download KitchenForge and start cooking smarter today.
